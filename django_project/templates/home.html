Orchestral Conducting

During my time at RIT, I became a member of RIT Game Symphony Orchestra, the largest performing arts group on RIT's campus, with around 180 performers. I have remained a member of this group since the Fall of 2020, and held several executive board positions while I was still a student.

From May 2022 to May 2023 I was the wind section lead and from May 2023 to May 2026 I was one of the orchestral conductor's of the group. I began as the assistant conductor and held that position for two years, followed by one year as the primary conductor.

In the months following, I have begun the process of starting my own community orchestra within the Rochester Metropolitan Area, known as the Rochester Contemporary Community Orchestra. I am establishing this group with fellow RIT and GSO alumni Ethan Geye, with him holding the role of president and myself as conductor.
