Airspace Sectorization via Voronoi Diagrams

Was one of three main developers on the project. My task was to develop an algorithm to sectorize a given airspace using a voronoi diagram based on restrictions simulated via a genetic algorithm.

Developed multithreaded raytracing engine that can render simple objects within 3D space. The entire thing was written from scratch and designed to modular such that new objects could be easily created and placed into the world.
